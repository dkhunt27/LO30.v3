Livonia Over 30 Hockey ANY DAY PLAYING HOCKEY IS A GREAT DAY!

A league built around fun, fair, and competitive play for all ages (over 30) and skill levels.

Why Livonia Over 30?

If you want to play hockey, you live in Livonia, and you are over 30, then you need to get into the Livonia Over 30 Hockey league. The league has been in existance since 1970. It is loaded with tradition, families, and fun. Through a unique rating system, draft process, and line structure, the league makes it fair and competitive for all ages (over 30) and skill levels. The facilities are great. The ice times are fantastic. And the cost is just right!

Join the League

Are you a Livonia Resident? Over 30 years old? Able to commit to playing hockey Thursday and Sunday nights? Interested in playing in the Best hockey league in Michigan? Add your name to the wait list! Send an email to information@livoniaover30hockey.com Include your full name, address, city, state, zip code, home phone number and work/cell phone number, and the name(s) of anybody currently playing in the league that know you, and a brief background of your hockey playing abilities (how many years you've played, where you last played, what position you play, approx. how good a player you are, etc.).

Sub in the League

Maybe you cannot meet all the criteria for joining the league, but you are still interested in subbing. Send an email to information@livoniaover30hockey.com Include all the required info for joining the league.

Sponsorships

If you are interested in sponsoring a team in league, please send an email to information@livoniaover30hockey.com Include your full name, contact phone number, and best time to reach you. You do not need to play hockey, although many of our sponsors are also league members.
